Custom crafting recipes

You can define custom crafting recipes for the special composter item. Here are a few examples:

Composter with redstone on top

recipe-shape:
  - "R"
  - "C"

recipe-ingredients:
  - "R=REDSTONE"
  - "C=COMPOSTER"

Composter surrounded with diamonds

recipe-shape:
  - "DDD"
  - "DCD"
  - "DDD"

recipe-ingredients:
  - "D=DIAMOND"
  - "C=COMPOSTER"
